About Me

Heyo. I’m Eileen, a copywriter and content strategist living in Seattle, WA. Look, I didn’t take the traditional route to copywriting. I’ve always been a writer, but I…uh…we’ll say fumbled with wielding it early on.

I didn’t major in advertising. I missed the boat on internships. And where a responsible greenhorn English grad would’ve armed herself against a beleaguered economy by enrolling in portfolio school, I decamped to Colorado to become the world’s worst waitress. This isn’t a humble brag. I’m not some self-glamorizing goof trying to rebrand my youthful flailing as a free- spirited rebellion against THE MAN, maaan. (takes bong rip.)

I’m telling you this because it’s true. And because at some point, fed up with choreographing interest in patrons’ meat preferences, I decided not writing was no longer an option. So, I wrote. I wrote for fledgling music festivals. I wrote for photographer friends. I wrote tremendously stupid blogs about space zombies that nobody read. And, eventually, I wrote my way into a job.

First, as an in-house writer at a natural gas company in Colorado, and currently as a copywriter at an agency in Seattle, WA where I write for brands like Microsoft and VMware. And you know what? Writing every day kicks ass. I love it. Even when writing about profoundly nerdy things like network freaking virtualization. Because I wasn’t always this lucky. (And also because I sucked ass at the whole waitressing thing.)
